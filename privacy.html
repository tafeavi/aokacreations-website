Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

AOKA Creations ("AOKA Creations", "we", "us" or "our") is an Australian studio that builds tools, resources and experiences supporting emotional wellbeing, reflection and personal growth — including the Next Moment app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across our website and products. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information with care.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on how you interact with us, this may include:

  • Contact information — such as your name and email address, only when you choose to contact us.
  • App account information — if you create an account in one of our apps, we may store an identifier and basic preferences needed to provide the service.
  • Reflections and entries — content you create within our apps. Where possible, this is stored securely and treated as private to you.
  • Usage and device data — anonymous or aggregated information such as app version, device type and crash diagnostics, used to keep our products reliable.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve our products and services;
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide support;
  • Understand how our products are used, so we can make them better;
  • Keep our products secure and diagnose technical issues.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your private reflections for advertising.

3. Legal basis and your consent

Where required, we rely on your consent and on our legitimate interest in operating and improving our products. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us or by ceasing to use the relevant product.

4. Sharing of information

We only share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our products (for example, secure hosting and analytics), and only to the extent necessary. These providers are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations. We may also disclose information where required by law.

5. Data storage and security

We use reputable infrastructure providers and apply technical and organisational measures designed to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. While no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, we work to safeguard your data and limit access to it.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see "Your rights" below).

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@aokacreations.com.

8. Children's privacy

Our products are not directed to children under the age required by local law without appropriate consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

9. International users

AOKA Creations is based in Australia. If you access our products from outside Australia, your information may be processed in Australia or in other countries where our service providers operate, under appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or apps where appropriate.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Privacy enquiries: privacy@aokacreations.com
  • General & support: support@aokacreations.com
